Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how JetPrintX collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

JetPrintX Inc. (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website jetprintx.com, purchase our products, or use our services (collectively, the “Services”).

We comply with applicable U.S. privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the CPRA, where relevant.

By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Personal identifiers: name, email address, phone number, shipping & billing address
  • Payment information: credit/debit card details, billing address (processed securely via third-party payment processors)
  • Commercial information: products purchased, order history, preferences
  • Internet / electronic activity: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent, referring URL
  • Geolocation data: approximate location derived from IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Customer support data: communications, chat transcripts, support tickets

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Process and fulfill orders, including shipping and payment handling
  • Provide customer service, support, and warranty assistance
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates)
  • Improve our website, products, and services (analytics & research)
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent or as permitted by law)
  • Detect fraud, enforce our terms, and protect our rights
  • Comply with legal obligations

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

We share personal information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (payment processors, shipping carriers, cloud hosting, analytics providers)
  • To comply with legal obligations, subpoenas, court orders, or government requests
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of JetPrintX, our users, or the public

We do not sell your personal information.

We implement commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use/share it
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights

To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@jetprintx.com.

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Order and payment data is typically retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the “Last updated” date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically.
