Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We’ve designed this policy to make returns and refunds simple and fair.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

JetPrintX is committed to your complete satisfaction with every purchase. This Return and Refund Policy applies to products purchased directly from jetprintx.com or our authorized U.S. sales channels. Please read it carefully.

To be eligible for a return, the item must meet all of the following conditions:

  • Returned within 30 calendar days of delivery date
  • In original, unopened packaging (unless defective)
  • Unused, undamaged, and in resalable condition
  • Accompanied by original proof of purchase (order number or invoice)
  • Includes all accessories, manuals, cables, and packaging materials

Products showing signs of use beyond basic inspection (test prints, removed protective films, etc.) may be subject to a restocking fee of up to 15% or denied return entirely.

The following items cannot be returned or refunded unless they arrive damaged or defective:

  • Ink cartridges, toner, and other consumables (opened or used)
  • Custom-configured or personalized printers
  • Opened software, firmware updates, or digital downloads
  • Items marked “Final Sale” or “Clearance” at time of purchase
  • Products damaged due to customer misuse, negligence, or unauthorized repair
  1. Contact us within 30 days of delivery at returns@jetprintx.com or 1-800-JET-PRINT. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will issue a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions (usually within 24 business hours).
  3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging (or equivalent), including all accessories and the RMA number clearly visible on the outside of the box.
  4. Ship the item to the address provided in the RMA instructions using a trackable shipping method.
  5. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund or exchange (typically within 5–10 business days).

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the return
  • PayPal / ACH: 3–7 business days
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective on arrival)

We reserve the right to refuse refunds or impose restocking fees if the product does not meet return conditions.

If you received a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, we will gladly arrange an exchange at no additional cost to you (including return shipping).

For change-of-mind exchanges (different model, color, etc.), standard return shipping fees apply and a restocking fee may be charged if the item has been used.

  • Original outbound shipping: Non-refundable (unless we sent the wrong/defective item)
  • Return shipping: Customer responsibility for change-of-mind returns
  • Defective / wrong item returns: We provide a prepaid return label or reimburse reasonable shipping costs
